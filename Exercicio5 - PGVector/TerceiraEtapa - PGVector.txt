5. Similaridade Cosseno:

SELECT 
    id,
    titulo,
    embedding,
    embedding <=> '[0.8, 0.1, 0.3]' AS distancia_cosseno
FROM documentos
ORDER BY embedding <=> '[0.8, 0.1, 0.3]'
LIMIT 3;
